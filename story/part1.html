Part 1: First message Levels 1-2

'start'

???:  Chargy, you have yet to complete your task.

Chargy:  Right; I still need to charge all the devices in goober world

???:  Teleporting you there now.

At this point, the player has no information about the world, and only a vague goal. "charge all the devices".
The player is then teleported to the first level of goober world, where they immediatly will see text saying how to move. And the only other thing is a phone. When they approach it, particles fly off chargy, and you see the screen fill up, then get teleported to the next level.
Note that unlike the player, Chargy is not clueless. He already knew what his goal was, and how to acheive it. Just needed a reminder from '???'.
